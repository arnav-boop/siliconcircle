About Us

The Silicon Circle was founded on the principle that the best way to learn is by doing. We are a fully student-run organization dedicated to creating a space for teens to explore their interests in STEM, coding, and technology. We emphasize collaboration, innovation, and a hands-on approach to learning. Our goal is to provide a platform for young minds to connect, create, and challenge themselves in a supportive, peer-led environment. We believe in the power of community to foster growth and inspire the next generation of innovators.

FAQs

Who can join?

Any teen between the ages of 13 and 19 with a passion for technology is welcome to apply.

Is there a membership fee?

No, the Silicon Circle is completely free for all members.

What kind of projects do you work on?

Our members work on a wide range of projects, from web and mobile apps to robotics and AI. The only limit is your imagination.

How often do you meet?

We have a monthly makeathon and various workshops and speaker sessions throughout the month. All events are announced on our community platform.
